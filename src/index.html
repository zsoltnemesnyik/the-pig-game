Rules

The goal is simple: roll the dice and reach the final score first.

  • Roll the dices by clicking on them.

  • Each roll gets added to the previous roll.

  • Click on HOLD, if you are satisfied with your current score.

  • If you roll a 1, it's the next player's turn.

  • If both of your dices are sixes, you lose your total score.
